# Client Intake Form

Send this to a new client after the contract is signed and before the kickoff call. Their answers inform the strategy brief and replace the need to ask the same questions on the call.

---

## Business Basics

**Business name:**

**Website URL:**

**What does your business do? (2-3 sentences, plain language):**

**How long have you been in business?**

**Where do you operate? (local, regional, national, remote/online):**

---

## Your Clients

**Who is your ideal client? Describe the specific person - not just their job title but their situation:**

**What is their biggest frustration that your business solves?**

**What have they usually tried before coming to you?**

**What makes your ideal client different from someone who is not a good fit?**

---

## Your Offer

**What is the name of the main thing you sell?**

**What does it cost?**

**What format is it? (done-for-you service, coaching, course, product, subscription, other):**

**What does a client get when they work with you? List the main components:**

**What result does a client typically get? Be specific (time, money, outcome):**

**How long does it take for a client to see results?**

**Do you offer a guarantee? If yes, describe it:**

---

## Your Current Situation

**How are you currently getting most of your clients?**

**How many leads do you get per month right now (approximate)?**

**How many new clients do you close per month?**

**What is your current monthly revenue (approximate)?**

**What is your revenue goal for the next 3-6 months?**

**What is the biggest thing standing between you and that goal?**

---

## Paid Advertising History

**Have you run paid ads before? (Facebook/Instagram, Google, other):**

**If yes, what worked and what did not?**

**Do you have an existing ad account? If yes, what is the account ID?**

**What is your starting monthly ad budget?**

---

## Content and Assets

**Do you have photos or videos of yourself, your team, or your work?**
(If yes, please share a Google Drive or Dropbox link)

**Do you have existing testimonials or case studies?**
(If yes, please share them or describe them here)

**Do you have a logo and brand colors?**
(If yes, please share files)

---

## Technology

**What CRM or contact management system do you use?**
(We default to GoHighLevel - let us know if you use something else)

**What do you use for your website or landing pages?**

**What email marketing tool do you use?**

**What calendar booking tool do you use?**

---

## Communication

**Who is the main point of contact for this engagement?**

**What is the best way to reach you? (email, phone, Slack, text):**

**What is your response time expectation for feedback and approvals?**

**Who needs to approve copy and creative before it goes live?**

---

## Anything Else

**Is there anything specific you want us to know about your business, your clients, or how you prefer to work?**

**Are there any topics, claims, or approaches you do not want us to use in copy or ads?**

---

*Thank you. We will review your answers before the kickoff call so we can spend our time on strategy, not gathering basics.*
