# Thumbnail Brief

## Purpose

Write the creative brief for a video thumbnail. Thumbnails are the primary factor in whether a YouTube video gets clicked. A strong thumbnail communicates a promise in 0.5 seconds and creates curiosity or desire to click. The output is a brief the Creative Design Agent or designer uses to produce the thumbnail without additional direction.

## Used By

- Creative Design Agent (primary)
- Video Agent (references when planning the video production)
- Social Media Agent (uses for Reel and TikTok cover frames)

## Trigger

Use when a YouTube video, long-form social video, or Reel needs a thumbnail or cover image.

## Inputs Needed

- Video title - required
- Video topic and main promise - required
- Target audience - required
- Brand visual rules - required
- Any footage available (for real photos of presenter or client) - helpful
- Platform (YouTube, Instagram Reel cover, TikTok) - required

## Process

**Step 1: Identify the three thumbnail elements**

A strong thumbnail has exactly three things:
1. **Tension or result** - What emotional state or outcome does the viewer want? Show it.
2. **Text overlay** - Maximum 5 words. Should complete or complement the title, not repeat it.
3. **Face or focal point** - A face (with clear expression) or a strong visual element that draws the eye

The thumbnail should stand alone without the title. If someone covers the title and still wants to click, the thumbnail is working.

**Step 2: Define the visual concept**

What is the primary image? Options:
- **Presenter face**: Works when the presenter is the brand or authority signal. Expression should match the emotion of the video (shocked, confident, concerned, excited).
- **Before/after**: Works for transformation content. Show the contrast.
- **Object or product**: Works when the subject is visual (a tool, a result, a place).
- **Text-dominant**: Works for high-trust brands where the title alone creates clicks.

**Step 3: Write the text overlay**

5 words maximum. The text should be:
- In contrast color against the background
- Large enough to read at thumbnail size (60px or smaller)
- A complement to the video title, not a repeat

Examples:
- Title: "How We Generated 47 Leads in 30 Days"
  Thumbnail text: "47 Leads in 30 Days"

- Title: "Why Your Facebook Ads Stopped Working"
  Thumbnail text: "Stop Making This Mistake"

**Step 4: Define the color strategy**

Thumbnail color should:
- Contrast with YouTube's white background (avoid pure white thumbnails)
- Use brand colors but with high contrast
- Have one dominant color that draws the eye

Red, orange, and yellow are high-attention. Blue and green are calming but lower scroll-stop.

**Step 5: Specify dimensions and production format**

| Platform | Dimensions | Format |
|---------|-----------|--------|
| YouTube | 1280x720 | JPG or PNG, under 2MB |
| Instagram Reel cover | 1080x1920 | PNG |
| TikTok cover | 1080x1920 | JPG or PNG |
| Facebook video thumbnail | 1200x628 | JPG |

## Output Format

```
THUMBNAIL BRIEF - [Client/Video Title] - [Date]

PLATFORM: [YouTube / Instagram Reel / TikTok / Facebook]
DIMENSIONS: [WxH]
VIDEO TITLE: [Full video title]
VIDEO PROMISE: [One sentence - what clicking this video gives the viewer]

---

VISUAL CONCEPT:

Primary element: [Face / Object / Before-after / Text-dominant]

Face direction (if face is primary):
  Person: [Name or description]
  Expression: [Specific emotion - shocked, confident, concerned, excited]
  Looking: [At camera / At object in frame / Directional toward the text]
  Photo needed: [New photo required / Use existing photo: describe which]

Visual element (if no face or as secondary):
  [Specific description of what to show and how]

Background:
  Color: [Hex or description]
  Texture/image: [Clean color / Blurred scene / Pattern]

---

TEXT OVERLAY:

Main text: "[5 words max]"
  Font size: [Large, dominant]
  Color: [Hex or contrast color name]
  Position: [Top / Bottom / Left / Right / Center]
  Style: [Bold / All caps / Mixed]

Supporting text (optional): "[If additional text]"
  Font size: [Smaller than main]
  Color: [...]
  Position: [...]

---

COLOR DIRECTION:

Primary thumbnail color: [Hex or description]
Text color: [Hex or description]
Contrast ratio: [High contrast required - text must be readable at 60px width]

---

THUMBNAIL TEST:

At small size (60px wide), this thumbnail should communicate:
"[One phrase - what a viewer takes away in 0.5 seconds]"

Competitor thumbnails to differentiate from: [What to avoid - common thumbnail styles in this niche]

---

REFERENCE:
[Link or description of any thumbnail reference for direction]
```

## Quality Check

- [ ] Text overlay is 5 words or fewer
- [ ] Text is readable at thumbnail size (60px)
- [ ] One dominant visual focal point
- [ ] High contrast between text and background
- [ ] Thumbnail communicates its promise without the video title
- [ ] Platform dimensions are specified
- [ ] Brief is complete enough to produce without follow-up questions
