# Social Post Copy

## Purpose

Write platform-native copy for individual social media posts: Instagram captions, Facebook posts, LinkedIn posts, and short-form captions for video content. The output is post-ready copy with hook, body, and CTA matched to platform norms. This is individual post copy, not a full content calendar (see content-calendar skill for that).

## Used By

- Copywriter (primary)
- Social Media Agent (hands off topic and brief, reviews output)
- QA Agent (reviews before publish)

## Trigger

Use when writing individual social posts or when the Social Media Agent needs copy for a specific piece of content.

## Inputs Needed

- Platform (Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, or short-form) - required
- Content pillar and topic - required
- Goal of the post (educate, drive link click, social proof, engagement) - required
- Audience profile or buyer persona - required
- Any proof points, stats, or stories to include - helpful
- Brand voice rules - required

## Process

**Step 1: Confirm the platform and length rules**

Platform copy behaves differently. Know the rules before writing.

| Platform | Optimal length | Line breaks | Hashtags | CTA style |
|----------|---------------|------------|---------|----------|
| Instagram | 50-150 words | Short paragraphs, white space | 3-5 in caption or first comment | "Link in bio" or engagement prompt |
| Facebook | 100-300 words | Short paragraphs | 0-2 optional | Direct link OK, engagement prompt or curiosity close |
| LinkedIn | 150-400 words | One idea per paragraph, lots of white space | 0-3 at end | Professional CTA, comment prompt, or DM invite |
| Short-form caption | 50-100 words | Minimal, hooks attention | 3-5 | Aligns with the spoken CTA in the video |

**Step 2: Write the hook first**

The first 1-2 lines are what appear before "more" on mobile. They must stop the scroll.

Six hook types to choose from:
1. Curiosity gap: "Most [niche] people don't know this about [topic]..."
2. Pain mirror: "You've probably tried [X] and it didn't work..."
3. Contrarian: "Stop doing [common advice]. Here's why..."
4. Bold statement: "I made [specific claim] and I can tell you exactly why..."
5. Direct benefit: "[Specific result] without [thing they dread]"
6. Social proof opener: "My client [result] in [timeframe]. Here's what changed..."

Write 2-3 hook options. Pick the strongest.

**Step 3: Write the body**

After the hook, deliver on what was promised. Different pillar types need different body structures:

- **Education posts**: Hook → single insight → proof or example → takeaway
- **Social proof posts**: Hook → client situation → what changed → result → what it means for reader
- **Brand/story posts**: Hook → honest moment or observation → what was learned → why it matters
- **Engagement posts**: Hook → provocative question or poll setup → clear engagement prompt
- **Offer posts**: Hook → pain point → offer reveal (specific, not vague) → CTA

One idea per post. Do not try to cover multiple points. The post should feel like a complete thought.

**Step 4: Write the CTA**

Every post needs a clear next action. CTAs should be specific to the goal:
- Link click: "Full breakdown in my bio link" or "DM me [word] and I'll send it over"
- Engagement: "Drop [emoji] if this resonates" or "What would you add? Tell me below"
- Lead gen: "If this sounds like where you're at, DM me '[keyword]'"
- Social proof: "Save this if it helped" or "Share with someone who needs to hear this"

Avoid generic CTAs like "Click here" or "Check it out."

**Step 5: Format for the platform**

- Instagram and short-form: break long sentences into single lines for visual rhythm
- Facebook: standard paragraphs, no over-formatting
- LinkedIn: one sentence or one short idea per line, double line break between paragraphs
- All platforms: read aloud before submitting. If it sounds stiff or corporate, rewrite.

## Output Format

```
SOCIAL POST COPY - [Client] - [Platform] - [Date]

CONTENT PILLAR: [Education / Social Proof / Brand / Engagement / Offer]
GOAL: [Educate / Drive link click / Generate engagement / Lead gen]

---

HOOK OPTIONS:
Option A: [Hook line]
Option B: [Hook line]
Option C: [Hook line]
Selected: [A / B / C]

---

FULL POST:

[Hook line]

[Body - platform-appropriate format]

[CTA]

[Hashtags if applicable]

---

COPY NOTES:
- Platform: [Instagram / Facebook / LinkedIn / Short-form]
- Word count: [X]
- CTA type: [Link / Engagement / Lead gen]
- Proof point used: [If applicable]
- Suggested publish time: [If Social Media Agent specified]
```

## Quality Check

- [ ] Hook appears in the first 1-2 lines, before the fold
- [ ] One idea, not multiple topics in the same post
- [ ] CTA is specific, not generic
- [ ] Copy length matches the platform norm
- [ ] No banned words or brand voice violations (run against voice-rules.md)
- [ ] No em dashes
- [ ] Reads naturally when spoken aloud
