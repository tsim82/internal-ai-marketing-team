# Facebook Post

## Purpose

Produce a complete, publish-ready Facebook post: caption with hook, body, CTA, and visual brief. Facebook posts behave differently than Instagram - they allow longer text, support link sharing better, and the algorithm rewards native content (content that does not push people off Facebook). This skill covers organic Facebook page posts, not paid ad copy.

## Used By

- Social Media Agent (briefs the topic and assigns)
- Copywriter (writes the post)
- Creative Design Agent (uses visual brief for the image)
- QA Agent (reviews before publish)

## Trigger

Use when a Facebook post slot in the content calendar needs copy.

## Inputs Needed

- Content pillar and topic - required
- Goal (educate, social proof, drive link click, engagement) - required
- Audience profile - required
- Brand voice rules - required
- Any specific proof points or stories to include - helpful

## Process

**Step 1: Understand Facebook organic behavior**

Facebook favors posts that:
- Generate comments (not just likes)
- Keep people on the platform (avoid external links in the post body - put links in comments or use native video)
- Are text-heavy when the content is strong
- Use a natural, conversational voice

Facebook users are slightly older and more mix of personal and professional content than Instagram. Tone can be slightly warmer and more personal.

**Step 2: Write the opening**

Facebook shows the first 2-3 lines before a "See more" cutoff. The opening must earn the click.

Strong Facebook openings:
- A short story setup: "Three years ago I was [situation]..."
- A direct question that targets the exact problem: "If you're running a service business and your pipeline feels unpredictable, this might be why..."
- A bold statement followed by a teaser: "Most agencies never tell you this about running Facebook ads. Here's what's actually happening..."

**Step 3: Write the body**

Facebook body ideal length: 100-300 words for most posts. Can go longer for story posts if the content is genuinely engaging.

Paragraph structure: 1-3 sentences per paragraph. Hit return often. Long blocks of text on Facebook get scrolled past.

Include either:
- A story with a specific situation, what changed, and what was learned
- A numbered list of points (Facebook users respond well to scannable lists)
- A case study snippet with a specific result

**Step 4: Handle links correctly**

If the post includes a link to an external page:
- Post the content without the link in the text body
- Paste the link in the first comment
- OR use Facebook's link preview but write the post text to work without the link

Putting links in the body reduces organic reach.

**Step 5: Write the CTA**

Facebook CTAs that work:
- "Drop a comment if this resonates - I read every one"
- "What would you add? Tell me below"
- "Share this with someone who needs to hear it"
- "Link to [X] is in the first comment"

Avoid: "Click here to buy now" as a native organic post CTA.

## Output Format

```
FACEBOOK POST - [Client] - [Date] - [Content Pillar]

GOAL: [Educate / Social proof / Engagement / Offer / Brand]
LINK INCLUDED: [Yes - in first comment / No]
CONTENT CALENDAR SLOT: [Day and date]

---

POST TEXT:

[Opening 2-3 lines - must hook before "See more"]

[Body - paragraphs with line breaks between]

[CTA]

---

FIRST COMMENT (if link is included):
"[Link URL]"
or
"[Short context line] → [Link URL]"

---

WORD COUNT: [X]

---

VISUAL BRIEF FOR CREATIVE DESIGN AGENT:

Image concept: [What to show]
Text overlay: [Yes - "text here" / No]
Format: [1200x630 landscape for link posts / 1080x1080 for non-link posts]
Tone/mood: [...]
```

## Quality Check

- [ ] First 2-3 lines work as a hook without needing "See more"
- [ ] No external links in the post body (link in first comment or excluded)
- [ ] Paragraphs are short with line breaks
- [ ] CTA encourages engagement native to the platform (comment, share)
- [ ] Tone is conversational and natural, not corporate
- [ ] No em dashes, no banned words
- [ ] Length: 100-300 words for standard posts, longer acceptable for strong story content
