# Repurposing System

## Purpose

Convert a single piece of long-form content into multiple platform-native assets. Repurposing multiplies the value of every piece of content created. One VSL, podcast, YouTube video, or blog post can become 8-15 assets if repurposed correctly. This skill defines the repurposing tiers and the process for executing each one.

## Used By

- Social Media Agent (primary - routes repurposed assets to the right outputs)
- Copywriter (writes repurposed text assets)
- Video Agent (repurposes video clips)
- Creative Design Agent (creates derivative visual assets)

## Trigger

Use whenever a long-form content piece is completed (YouTube video, podcast episode, webinar, VSL, long blog post) and needs to be converted into social and short-form assets.

## Inputs Needed

- Original long-form content (video, audio, or written) - required
- Transcript (if video or audio) - required or needed before starting
- Platform distribution plan (which platforms are active for this client) - required
- Key moments or insights identified from the content - helpful

## Process

**Step 1: Identify the key moments**

Before repurposing, identify 3-5 standout moments in the original content:
- The strongest insight or claim
- A specific story or case study
- A contrarian take or surprising statement
- A practical step or framework
- A client result or testimonial

These key moments become the seed for every repurposed asset.

**Step 2: Apply the repurposing tier system**

**Tier 1 - Long-form video (YouTube, webinar, VSL):**
Produces 8+ assets:

| Asset | What it is |
|-------|-----------|
| 3-5 short-form clips | 30-90 second standalone clips pulled from the best moments |
| 3-5 short-form captions | Caption copy for each clip |
| 1 long-form summary LinkedIn post | Main takeaways written as a LinkedIn post |
| 1-2 Instagram carousels | Key framework or list from the video turned into slides |
| 2-3 quote graphics | Strong statements pulled from the transcript, formatted as shareable images |
| 1 email newsletter | Video summary with link to the full video |
| 1-2 Twitter/X thread starters | If platform is active |

**Tier 2 - Long-form written (blog post, case study, guide):**
Produces 5+ assets:

| Asset | What it is |
|-------|-----------|
| 1 LinkedIn post | Main argument or insight from the piece |
| 1-2 Instagram posts | One insight per post, pulled from the piece |
| 1 Facebook post | The story or key finding, written conversationally |
| 1-2 carousels | If the piece has a list or framework |
| 1 short-form video script | The hook and main point of the written piece, adapted to video |

**Tier 3 - Short-form video (Reel, TikTok, Short):**
Produces 4 assets:

| Asset | What it is |
|-------|-----------|
| Cross-post to all short-form platforms | Same clip, platform-native caption per platform |
| 1 LinkedIn post | Written version of the video's main point |
| 1 quote graphic | One key line from the video |

**Step 3: Create the repurposing task list**

For each asset, assign:
- Asset type
- Source moment from the original content
- Who creates it (Video Agent for clips, Copywriter for text, Creative Design for graphics)
- Platform and format
- Due date

```
| [Continue] | [...] | [...] | [...] | [...] | [...] |

TEXT ASSETS:
| # | Asset type | Source | Platform | Owner | Due |
|---|-----------|--------|---------|-------|-----|

|---|-----------|--------|---------|-------|-----|

```

## Quality Check

- [ ] Key moments are identified before assets are assigned
- [ ] Every asset has a named source moment from the original content (not "pull something from the video")
- [ ] Each asset has an owner and a due date
- [ ] Assets are written natively for each platform (not the same text copy-pasted across platforms)
- [ ] Short-form clips get platform-specific captions, not one generic caption
