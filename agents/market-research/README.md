# Market Research Agent

## What This Agent Does

Produces audience profiles, competitor research, hook lists, objection maps, and research briefs. Feeds intelligence to the Copywriter, Offer Agent, and Paid Ads Agent.

## When To Use It

- Before writing copy for any campaign
- Before building or refining an offer
- When entering a new niche
- When campaigns are underperforming and new angles are needed
