## Target Audience

**Primary audience:** Atlanta-area homeowners, ages 35-65, homes 12+ years old, household income $80,000+

**Demographics:**
- Age range: 35-65
- Gender split: Roughly even (decisions are household decisions)
- Income: $80,000-$200,000 household
- Location: Atlanta suburbs (not downtown - single family homes)

**Psychographics:**
- What they want: Peace of mind that their home is protected, not to deal with leaks or insurance headaches
- What they fear: Getting ripped off by a contractor, having to re-do the work, damage to their home interior
- What has failed them before: Hired a cheap contractor who cut corners; filed an insurance claim that went poorly; got quotes and then didn't know who to trust
- Where they spend time online: Facebook, NextDoor, YouTube home improvement content, Google search when something breaks

**Awareness level:** Problem aware (they know they have a roofing issue) → Solution aware (they know they need a contractor, not sure which one)

## Goals and KPIs

**Monthly revenue goal (from paid leads):** $120,000 (10 jobs × $12,000)
**Monthly leads needed:** 40 (to get 10 jobs at target conversion rates)
**Target CPL:** $80-$100
**Target close rate:** 25% of qualified calls
**Target CPA:** $500 or less (budget per closed job)

**Funnel math:**
- Budget: $4,000/month
- Target CPL: $90
- Expected leads: 44
- Lead-to-booked rate: 50% → 22 booked estimates
- Show rate: 80% → 18 on-site estimates
- Close rate: 55% → 10 jobs
- Revenue: 10 × $12,000 = $120,000
- ROAS: $120,000 ÷ $4,000 = 30x
