# AI Marketing Team - Skool Classroom Layout v2
## Plain Language, Easy to Follow

Teaching approach: Every lesson assumes the student knows nothing about AI or marketing tools. Show every click. Explain every step. No jargon without an explanation. If something could confuse someone, slow down and explain it.

## Recording Notes

- Talk to one person. Imagine a local roofer who's never touched AI sitting next to you.
- Say what you're clicking before you click it.
- When something could go wrong, say what to do if it does.
- Short is better. 8-10 minutes per lesson is the sweet spot.
- If a lesson runs over 12 minutes, split it into two.
