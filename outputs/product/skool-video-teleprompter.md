## Filming Notes

- Read slow. You'll think you're going too slow. You're not.
- Pause at every blank line.
- Look into the lens, not the screen.
- Film 2-3 takes. Use the one where you sound most natural, not most polished.
- Clean background. Good light on your face. That's all you need.
