## Testing Notes

**Priority order:**
1. Angle 2 (Productivity) - most relatable pain, works across all three audiences
2. Angle 1 (Story) - best for building trust and connection, strongest for video
3. Angle 5 (Short Hook) - fastest to film, good for Reels/short-form video testing
4. Angle 3 (Contrarian) - works with a warm-ish audience or one that's seen other AI content
5. Angle 4 (Price Comparison) - strongest if targeting people already paying for agencies

**Format recommendation:**
- Film Angle 1 and Angle 5 as talking head videos first
- Angle 2 and 4 work well as static image with Tyrone + headline text overlay
- Lead with Tyrone's face in the creative - personal brand ads outperform faceless brand at this price point

**Targeting recommendation:**
- Interest: Claude AI, ChatGPT, AI tools, digital marketing, agency owners, business owners
- Behavior: Small business owners
- Lookalike: Build from email list once you have 100+ members
- Start with $50-100/day, 3 ad sets, 1 angle each
- Kill at 2x CPL with no conversions after 3 days
- Scale winner by 20% every 7 days
