## Objections and Responses

**"I'm not technical."**
You don't need to be. The setup guide walks you through every step. If you can follow instructions, you can get this running.

**"I already use ChatGPT."**
ChatGPT gives you one general assistant. This gives you 15 agents, each trained for a specific marketing job, with skill files, frameworks, and templates already built in.

**"Why do I need this if I have an agency?"**
You might not need to cancel your agency. But most members find they can replace a significant portion of what they're paying for - or at least stop depending on an agency for everything.

**"What if I cancel?"**
You keep anything you've already built. The agents are installed on your machine. You lose access to new updates and the community.
