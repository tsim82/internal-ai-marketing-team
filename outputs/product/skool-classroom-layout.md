## Recording Order Recommendation

Record in this order - not classroom order. Gets you to a sellable product fastest:

1. Section 1 (Start Here) - sets the frame, short to film
2. Section 2 (Getting Started) - most asked question from new members
3. Section 8 (Weekly Rhythm) - shows the ongoing value, great for marketing
4. Section 6 (Launch Your Ads) - what most people are buying this for
5. Section 5 (Create Your Content) - most lessons, film over multiple sessions
6. Section 3 (Research Your Market) - foundational, can reference in ads section
7. Section 4 (Build Your Offer) - shorter section, film in one session
8. Section 7 (Wire Your Automations) - most technical, film last

**Minimum viable launch:** Sections 1, 2, and one of 3-8. You can release the rest over the first 60 days as a founding member benefit.
