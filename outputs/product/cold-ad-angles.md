## Testing Notes

**Priority order to test:**
1. Angle 1 (Agency Bill Pain) - strongest cost comparison, clear target
2. Angle 2 (Solo Operator Pain) - speaks to the "no system" problem
3. Angle 4 (Short Direct Benefit) - good for video hook testing
4. Angle 3 (Proof First) - works well if you have a face/personal brand behind this
5. Angle 5 (Comparison) - strong but needs the right audience targeting to land

**Format recommendation:**
- Start with single image static ads for fastest testing
- Test hooks in the first line - these 5 hooks are the variable to validate
- Once a hook wins, expand to video with that same hook as the opening line

**Targeting recommendation (cold):**
- Interest: Claude AI, ChatGPT, AI tools, digital marketing, business owners, agency owners
- Behavior: Small business owners, online purchasers
- Exclude: Current customers once you have them
- Start broad and let Meta find the buyer - $50-100/day test budget across 3 angles
