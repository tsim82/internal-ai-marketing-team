# Budget Plan - SkyLine Roofing Co.
**Date:** 2026-06-19 | **Platform:** Meta Ads | **Market:** Residential Roofing, Atlanta GA

---

## Assumptions

All rates are client-provided benchmarks (no historical data yet). Replace with actuals as soon as data exists.

- Average job value: $12,000
- LP conversion rate (visitor to lead): 30%
- Lead-to-booked rate: 50%
- Show/appointment rate: 80%
- Close rate (showed to closed): 55%
- Revenue goal: $120,000/month (10 jobs)
- Proposed budget: $4,000/month
- Gross margin: 40%

---

## 1. Reverse-Engineered Funnel Math

**Step 1 - Jobs needed**
$120,000 revenue goal / $12,000 average job value = 10 jobs needed

**Step 2 - Showed appointments needed**
10 jobs / 55% close rate = 19 showed appointments needed

**Step 3 - Booked appointments needed**
19 showed / 80% show rate = 24 booked appointments needed

**Step 4 - Leads needed**
24 booked / 50% lead-to-booked rate = 48 leads needed

**Step 5 - Landing page visitors needed**
48 leads / 30% LP conversion rate = 160 visitors needed

**Funnel Summary**

| Stage | Volume Needed |
|---|---|
| Jobs closed | 10 |
| Showed appointments | 19 |
| Booked appointments | 24 |
| Leads | 48 |
| LP visitors | 160 |

---

## 2. Does the $4,000 Budget Work?

**Required CPL at $4,000:**
$4,000 / 48 leads = $83.33 CPL required

Meta benchmark for home services: $25-$80 CPL

The required $83 CPL sits just above the top of the typical Meta home services range. It's tight.

**ROAS check:**
$120,000 revenue / $4,000 spend = 30x ROAS

**Verdict:** The math is borderline. Works only if CPL holds at or below $83. No room for underperformance. Treat $4,000 as the floor, not the target.

---

## 3. Minimum Viable Budget

**Data minimum (20 leads):**
20 leads x $83 CPL = $1,660/month

**Budget to hit revenue goal with no buffer:**
48 leads x $83 CPL = $4,000/month

**Recommended minimum with 25% CPL buffer:**
$83 CPL x 1.25 = $104 CPL
48 leads x $104 CPL = $4,992/month

**Recommended minimum viable budget: $5,000/month**

---

## 4. Max Allowable CPA at 40% Gross Margin

Gross profit per job: $12,000 x 40% = $4,800

| Allocation to CAC | Max CPA |
|---|---|
| 100% of margin (break even) | $4,800 |
| 50% of margin | $2,400 |
| 25% of margin (healthy) | $1,200 |

**Projected actual CPA:** $4,000 / 10 jobs = $400

**Max allowable CPA recommendation: $1,200** (25% of gross margin). Projected $400 CPA is well within range if funnel performs.

---

## 5. Economic Flags

**Flag 1 - $4,000 budget has no CPL buffer**
If CPL drifts to $100, the same budget buys only 40 leads, producing 8 jobs ($96,000 revenue -- a $24,000 miss). Recommend $5,000/month or adjusting the month 1 goal to 8 jobs.

What $4,000 supports at $100 CPL:
- 40 leads x 50% booked = 20 booked
- 20 x 80% showed = 16 showed
- 16 x 55% closed = 8 jobs = $96,000 revenue

**Flag 2 - 55% close rate is unverified**
If close rate drops to 40% at the same budget:
- 48 leads produce only 7 jobs = $84,000 revenue (a $36,000 gap)
Verify close rate in month 1 before scaling spend.

**Flag 3 - 30% LP conversion is optimistic**
Meta home services LPs typically convert at 15-25%. A 30% rate requires a well-built, niche-specific page with strong local social proof. A generic template will not hit this. Route LP build to the appropriate agent before launch.

**Flag 4 - 30x ROAS creates fragile expectations**
This requires every funnel stage to perform simultaneously at benchmark or above. Set client expectations at 6-8 jobs in month 1, scaling to 10 by month 2-3 as the campaign optimizes.

## Summary Verdict

| Question | Answer |
|---|---|
| Does $4,000 work at these benchmarks? | Barely -- requires $83 CPL with zero buffer |
| Minimum viable budget | $5,000/month recommended |
| Max allowable CPA (25% of margin) | $1,200 |
| Projected actual CPA | $400 (excellent if funnel holds) |
| Unit economics | Strong -- $4,800 gross profit per job gives real room |
| Biggest risk | Close rate and LP conversion are unverified assumptions |

**Recommendation:** Start at $5,000/month if the client can stretch. If $4,000 is the hard ceiling, set the month 1 target at 8 jobs ($96,000) and plan to scale spend in month 2 once CPL data is confirmed. Do not promise $120,000 against a budget with no cushion.
